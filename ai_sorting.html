AI Sorting Game

This is a private capstone project. I wanted to put it on Itch along with my other games, but the size is too big due to the MLAgents package. This capstone project was created to use both of my track skills, AI and Game Design, in one large project. I created a simple ball sorting game and a simple AI, using reinforcement learning, to teach the AI how to play the game. The AI strated out with one colored ball, and once it learned that sufficiently, I would add another color to the game.
